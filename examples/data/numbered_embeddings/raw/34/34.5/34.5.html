§34.5(ii) Symmetry

Notes:
See Edmonds (1974, pp. 94, 95).
Keywords:
Regge symmetries, 6⁢j symbols
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/34.5.ii
See also:

The 6⁢j symbol is invariant under interchange of any two columns and also under interchange of the upper and lower arguments in each of any two columns, for example,

34.5.8 {j1j2j3l1l2l3}={j2j1j3l2l1l3}={j1l2l3l1j2j3}.

Equations (34.5.9) and (34.5.10) are called Regge symmetries. Additional symmetries are obtained by applying (34.5.8) to (34.5.9) and (34.5.10). See Srinivasa Rao and Rajeswari (1993, pp. 102–103) and references given there.
