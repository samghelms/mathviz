For the polynomials Cn(λ)⁡(x) and Pn(λ)⁡(x;ϕ) see §§18.3 and 18.19, respectively.

See Bo and Wong (1996) for an asymptotic expansion of Pn(12)⁡(cos⁡(n-12⁢θ);a,b) as n→∞, with a and b fixed. This expansion is in terms of the Airy function Ai⁡(x) and its derivative (§9.2), and is uniform in any compact θ-interval in (0,∞). Also included is an asymptotic approximation for the zeros of Pn(12)⁡(cos⁡(n-12⁢θ);a,b).
