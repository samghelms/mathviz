Errata (effective with 1.0.10):
The first paragraph of this section has been rewritten to correct reported errors. The constants C=(-27⁢q24⁢p3)1/2 and D=-(27⁢q24⁢p3)1/2, formerly given in Eq. (4.43.1) for use in Cases (a), (b), and (c) of Eq. (4.43.2), have been eliminated, and these three cases have been corrected. Formerly they read
  1. (a)

    A⁢sin⁡a, A⁢sin⁡(a+23⁢π), and A⁢sin⁡(a+43⁢π), with sin⁡(3⁢a)=C, when p<0 and C≤1.

  2. (b)

    A⁢cosh⁡a, A⁢cosh⁡(a+23⁢π⁢i), and A⁢cosh⁡(a+43⁢π⁢i), with cosh⁡(3⁢a)=C, when p<0 and C>1.

  3. (c)

    B⁢sinh⁡a, B⁢sinh⁡(a+23⁢π⁢i), and B⁢sinh⁡(a+43⁢π⁢i), with sinh⁡(3⁢a)=D, when p>0.

Reported 2014-10-31 by Masataka Urago
