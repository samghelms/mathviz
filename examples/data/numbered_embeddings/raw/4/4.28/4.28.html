Periodicity and Zeros

Keywords:
hyperbolic functions, trigonometric functions
See also:
info for 4.28

The functions sinh⁡z and cosh⁡z have period 2⁢π⁢i, and tanh⁡z has period π⁢i. The zeros of sinh⁡z and cosh⁡z are z=i⁢k⁢π and z=i⁢(k+12)⁢π, respectively, k∈ℤ.
