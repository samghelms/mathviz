§4.38(i) Power Series

Notes:
For (4.38.1) expand (1+z2)-1/2 by the binomial theorem and integrate term by term. For (4.38.2), write (1+z2)-1/2=z-1⁢(1+(1/z2))-1/2, ℜ⁡z>0, and then expand and integrate. For the constant of integration note that for large z, arcsinh⁡z behaves like ln⁡(2⁢z) and the constant is ln⁡2. (4.38.3) is proved similarly. (4.38.4)–(4.38.7) follow from the corresponding series for inverse trigonometric functions in §4.24.
