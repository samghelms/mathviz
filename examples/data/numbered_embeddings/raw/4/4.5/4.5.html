§4.5(i) Logarithms

Notes:
(4.5.1) and (4.5.5) can be verified by the methods of Hardy et al. (1967, pp. 106–107). (4.5.2) and (4.5.4) follow from (4.5.1). (4.5.3) follows from the fact that x=0 and x=0.5828⁢… are successive zeros of 32⁢x+ln⁡(1-x). (4.5.6) is obtained from the Maclaurin expansion of ln⁡(1+z).

§4.5(ii) Exponentials

Notes:
(4.5.7) to (4.5.12) are obtained by exponentiating the inequalities (4.5.1) and (4.5.2). For (4.5.13), see Hardy et al. (1967, p. 102). (4.5.14) follows from the fact that 1-12⁢x-e-x has 0 and 1.5936⁢… as consecutive zeros. (4.5.15) and (4.5.16) can be derived from the Maclaurin expansion of ez.
