Let δ denote an arbitrary small positive constant. Also let a,b,z be real or complex and fixed, and at least one of the following conditions be satisfied:

  • (a)

    a and/or b∈{0,-1,-2,…}.

  • (b)

    ℜ⁡z<12 and |c+n|≥δ for all n∈{0,1,2,…}.

  • (c)

    ℜ⁡z=12 and |ph⁡c|≤π-δ.

  • (d)

    ℜ⁡z>12 and α--12⁢π+δ≤ph⁡c≤α++12⁢π-δ, where

    15.12.1 α±=arctan⁡(ph⁡z-ph⁡(1-z)∓πln⁡|1-z-1|),
    Symbols:
    arctan⁡z: arctangent function, ln⁡z: principal branch of logarithm function, ph: phase, z: complex variable and α±
