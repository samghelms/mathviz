Notes:
See Olver (1997b, p. 404).

Permalink:

As x increases from -∞ to 0 each of the functions M⁡(x), M′⁡(x), |x|-1/4⁢N⁡(x), M⁡(x)⁢N⁡(x), θ′⁡(x), ϕ′⁡(x) is increasing, and each of the functions |x|1/4⁢M⁡(x), θ⁡(x), ϕ⁡(x) is decreasing.

In (9.8.20) and (9.8.21) the remainder after n terms does not exceed the (n+1)th term in absolute value and is of the same sign, provided that n≥0 for (9.8.20) and n≥1 for (9.8.21).

For higher terms in (9.8.22) and (9.8.23) see Fabijonas et al. (2004). Also, approximate values (25S) of the coefficients of the powers x-15, x-18, …, x-56 are available in Sherry (1959).
