Notes:
For (20.10.1) and (20.10.3) use §20.7(viii) with appropriate changes of integration variable. For (20.10.2) use (20.2.3) with z=0, τ=i⁢t, Bellman (1961, pp. 28–32), Koblitz (1993, pp. 70–75), and/or Titchmarsh (1986b, §2.6).

§20.10(ii) Laplace Transforms with respect to the Lattice Parameter

Let s, ℓ, and β be constants such that ℜ⁡s>0, ℓ>0, and sinh⁡|β|≤ℓ. Then

20.10.4 ∫0∞e-s⁢t⁢θ1⁡(β⁢π2⁢ℓ|i⁢π⁢tℓ2)⁢dt=∫0∞e-s⁢t⁢θ2⁡((1+β)⁢π2⁢ℓ|i⁢π⁢tℓ2)⁢dt=-ℓs⁢sinh⁡(β⁢s)⁢sech⁡(ℓ⁢s),
Symbols:
θj⁡(z|τ): theta function, dx: differential of x, e: base of exponential function, sech⁡z: hyperbolic secant function, sinh⁡z: hyperbolic sine function, ∫: integral, s: parameter, β: parameter and ℓ: parameter
