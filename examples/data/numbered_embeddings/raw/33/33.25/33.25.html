§33.25 Approximations

Keywords:
Coulomb functions: variables ρ,η, Coulomb phase shift
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/33.25
See also:
info for 33

Cody and Hillstrom (1970) provides rational approximations of the phase shift σ0⁡(η)=ph⁡Γ⁡(1+i⁢η) (see (33.2.10)) for the ranges 0≤η≤2, 2≤η≤4, and 4≤η≤∞. Maximum relative errors range from 1.09×10⁻²⁰ to 4.24×10⁻¹⁹.
