§33.1 Special Notation

Referenced by:
§33.24, §33.5(iv)
Permalink:

info for 33

(For other notation see Notation for the Special Functions.)

k,ℓ nonnegative integers.
r,x real variables.
ρ nonnegative real variable.
ϵ,η real parameters.
ψ⁡(x) logarithmic derivative of Γ⁡(x); see §5.2(i).
δ⁡(x) Dirac delta; see §1.17.
primes derivatives with respect to the variable.

The main functions treated in this chapter are first the Coulomb radial functions Fℓ⁡(η,ρ), Gℓ⁡(η,ρ), Hℓ±⁡(η,ρ) (Sommerfeld (1928)), which are used in the case of repulsive Coulomb interactions, and secondly the functions f⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r), h⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r), s⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r), c⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) (Seaton (1982, 2002a)), which are used in the case of attractive Coulomb interactions.

Alternative Notations

Keywords:
