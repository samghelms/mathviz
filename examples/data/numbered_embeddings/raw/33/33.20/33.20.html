§33.20(iv) Uniform Asymptotic Expansions

Keywords:
Coulomb functions: variables r,ϵ
Referenced by:
§2.8(iii), §2.8(iv), §33.12(ii)
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/33.20.iv
See also:
info for 33.20

For a comprehensive collection of asymptotic expansions that cover f⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) and h⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) as ϵ→0± and are uniform in r, including unbounded values, see Curtis (1964a, §7). These expansions are in terms of elementary functions, Airy functions, and Bessel functions of orders 2⁢ℓ+1 and 2⁢ℓ+2.
