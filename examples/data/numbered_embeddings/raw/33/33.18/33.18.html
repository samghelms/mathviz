§33.18 Limiting Forms for Large ℓ

Notes:
Combine (33.5.8) and (33.16.1), (33.16.2). For f⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) (33.19.1) can also be used.
Keywords:
Coulomb functions: variables r,ϵ
Referenced by:
§33.23(iv)
Permalink:

As ℓ→∞ with ϵ and r (≠0) fixed,

33.18.1 f⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) ∼(2⁢r)ℓ+1(2⁢ℓ+1)!,
h⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) ∼(2⁢ℓ)!π⁢(2⁢r)ℓ.
Symbols:
f⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r): regular Coulomb function, h⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r): irregular Coulomb function, ∼: asymptotic equality, !: factorial (as in n!), ℓ: nonnegative integer, r: real variable and ϵ: real parameter
