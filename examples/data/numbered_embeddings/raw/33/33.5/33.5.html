§33.5(i) Small ρ

Notes:
See Yost et al. (1936).
Keywords:
Coulomb functions: variables ρ,η
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/33.5.i
See also:

As ρ→0 with η fixed,

33.5.1 Fℓ⁡(η,ρ) ∼Cℓ⁡(η)⁢ρℓ+1,
Fℓ′⁡(η,ρ) ∼(ℓ+1)⁢Cℓ⁡(η)⁢ρℓ.
Symbols:
Cℓ⁡(η): normalizing constant for Coulomb radial functions, ∼: asymptotic equality, Fℓ⁡(η,ρ): regular Coulomb radial function, ℓ: nonnegative integer, ρ: nonnegative real variable and η: real parameter

ℓ=0,1,2,…,
Gℓ′⁡(η,ρ) ∼-ℓ⁢ρ-ℓ-1(2⁢ℓ+1)⁢Cℓ⁡(η),
ℓ=1,2,3,….
